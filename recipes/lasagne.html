Beef Lasagne

Description

A simple recipe for a rich beef lasagne.

Prep Time: 2 hours

Serves 6

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 750 g lean beef mince
  • 90 g prosciutto
  • 800 g passata
  • 200 mL beef stock
  • 1 tsp grated nutmeg
  • 300 g fresh lasagne sheets
  • 500 g white sauce
  • 125 g mozzeralla

Steps

  1. To make the meat sauce, heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a frying pan and cook 750g lean beef mince in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.
  2. Finely chop 45 g prosciutto, then stir through the meat mixture.
  3. Pour over 800g passata and 200ml hot beef stock. Add a little grated nutmeg, then season.
  4. Bring up to the boil, then simmer for 30 mins until the sauce looks rich.
  5. Heat oven to 180 °C/fan or 160 °C/gas 4 and lightly oil an ovenproof dish (about 30 x 20 cm).
  6. Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets. Drizzle over the white sauce.
  7. Repeat until you have 3 or more layers of pasta. Cover with the remaining white sauce, making sure you can't see any pasta poking through.
  8. Scatter 125g torn mozzarella over the top.
  9. Arrange the rest of the prosciutto on top. Bake for 45 mins until the top is bubbling and lightly browned.
